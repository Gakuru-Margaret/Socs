System Modules

Four Modules.
One Digital Ledger.

Every feature designed for field staff — cleaners, watchmen, and office assistants — on basic smartphones.

01
🕐

Digital Attendance

One-tap Clock-In / Clock-Out with server timestamps. No more ghost signing. Every entry is permanent and verifiable.

  • GPS-verified timestamps
  • Shift history & summaries
  • Late arrival flags
02
📋

Duty Checklist

Daily chores assigned per role. Staff tick off tasks as they go. Supervisors see real-time progress.

  • Role-assigned task lists
  • Auto-reset each morning
  • Photo proof upload
03
📦

Supply & Tools Ledger

Report shortages of sugar, water, tissue, mops, or towels. Managers approve requests in seconds.

  • Pantry & cleaning supplies
  • One-tap shortage alerts
  • Request approval workflow
04
🚨

Incident Logger

Watchmen report breakages or security alerts instantly. Upload photo evidence. Supervisors notified in real time.

  • Categorized incident reports
  • Photo evidence upload
  • Real-time Socket.IO alerts
05
📊

Supervisor Dashboard

A master view showing live attendance, task completion rates, pending supply requests, and open incidents — all from one screen. Manage by exception, not by walking every room.
